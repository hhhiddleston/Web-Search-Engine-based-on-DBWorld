CIKM 2018 - Call for participation
==================================
http://www.cikm2018.units.it/

The 27th ACM International Conference on Information and Knowledge Management (CIKM 2018), held during October 22-26, 2018 in Turin, Italy, at Centro Congressi Lingotto. Complete information is available at: http://www.cikm2018.units.it/ 

The CIKM 2018 theme is “From Big Data and Big Information to Big Knowledge”. Strategically positioned at the intersection of research on the management of knowledge, information, and data, CIKM is uniquely situated to highlight technologies and insights that truly materialize the Big Data, Big Information and Big Knowledge vision of the future. CIKM 2018 gives researchers and practitioners a unique opportunity to share their perspectives with others interested in the various aspects of these emerging research topics. The call for papers attracted submissions from all over the world.

CIKM 2018 comprises the following three keynote speakers, whose valuable and insightful talks will guide us to a better understanding of the future:

-"Shifting Information Interactions", Maarten de Rijke, University of Amsterdam, Netherlands;
-"Teaching Artificial Agents to Understand Language by Modelling Reward", Edward Grefenstette, DeepMind and University College London, United Kingdom;
-"Alexa and her Shopping Journey", Yoelle Maarek, Amazon, Israel.

Come in Turin and enjoy CIKM 2018!
