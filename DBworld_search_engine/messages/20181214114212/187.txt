     

The Living Analytics Research Centre (LARC) is a research centre set up in Singapore Management University (SMU) with Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) as our strategic partner. 
LARC conducts research on urban and social sensing, social-physical analytics and social activation that enable a society to become (i) personalized, for improving people’s lifestyle and well-being, and (ii) participatory, for getting people to perform tasks for public good. 
LARC has its research projects covering four important application domains, namely:
a) Urban and Community Livability, 
b) Jobs and Skills Intelligence, 
c) Personalised Urban Mobility, and 
d) Smart Consumption and Healthy Lifestyle
