The Institute of Informatics & Telecommunications of NCSR Demokritos, in Athens, Greece, invites applications for a junior and a senior Research Assistant in the Complex Event Recognition group. 

The research will focus on the following areas of Big Data analytics:

* Artificial Intelligence
* Machine Learning
* Data stream analytics
* Complex Event Processing
* Query Processing and Optimisation

The methods developed in the group are used to provide solutions in many application domains, including life sciences, maritime monitoring and financial engineering. Information on the research activities of the Complex Event Recognition group may be found at:

http://cer.iit.demokritos.gr

or by contacting Alexander Artikis (http://users.iit.demokritos.gr/~a.artikis/).

The successful candidate will have strong programming skills, proficiency in English, as well as an advanced degree in Computer Science. For the senior position, the successful candidate will additionally have good knowledge in one or more of the aforementioned areas, and a Ph.D. in Computer Science.

Interested candidates should send their CVs or queries by email to

gram_skel@iit.demokritos.gr

by January 10, 2019, mentioning 'CER junior' or 'CER senior' in the subject.

Shortlisted candidates will be invited to an interview in January.
