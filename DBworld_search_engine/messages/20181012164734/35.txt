PhD Open Position – Advertisement
*********************************
Swinburne University of Technology, Melbourne, Australia

***Title: Open PhD position in Internet of Things, Mobile and Edge Computing***

Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Swinburne University of Technology, Melbourne, Australia, invites outstanding candidates to apply for open PhD position in the area of Internet of Things, Mobile and Edge computing.

***Commencement Date: Successful applicants for this PhD position must commence by end of 2018***

***Value and duration:

The scholarship is valued at approx ~AU$27,000 per annum for three years (with a possibility of a six months extension).
The selected candidate will also receive a tuition-fee waiver.

***Eligibility

To be eligible for this scholarship you must:
* Meet Swinburne’s PhD entry requirements.
* Have highly competitive grades.
* Show evidence of research ability, i.e., have an honours degree, a master thesis, publications, or research training.
* Have research interests in the Internet of things, Context-aware computing, Mobile and Edge Computing, and/or Machine Learning.
* Have interest to perform multi-disciplinary research and work in a team.
* Possess strong programming skills (experience programming in one of the following: C/C++, Java, Python, Ruby, Go, Scala).

***How to Apply

To apply for the scholarship, please send an expression of interest with the following information to pjayaraman@swin.edu.au:
* A detailed curriculum vitae with publications (if any)
* Undergraduate and honours/master transcripts
* A covering letter providing a short description of topics that you will be interested in the Internet of Things, Mobile and Edge computing, and/or Machine Learning area (no more than 2 pages).
* For international students from non-English speaking countries, when submitting the application to Swinburne system, a proof of English proficiency may also be required (IELTS or TOEFL)
