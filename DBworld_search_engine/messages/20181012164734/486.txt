Eduopen video course on Database modeling and design
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
I announce that starting August 27, 2018, on www.eduopen.org the first edition of the video course on  Database modeling and design by Carlo Batini will be offered. Topics are:

- ER model
- Relational model
- Conceptual (database) design
- Logical design
 
No prerequisite is needed, except basic knowledge on set theory and functions. The access is free, at end participants get a certification. 

In case participants need tutorship, they have to register before August 27, otherwise the course can be attended in self-paced mode. 
 
 The written transcriptions of the lessons can be freely downloaded at
 
http://hdl.handle.net/10281/97114
