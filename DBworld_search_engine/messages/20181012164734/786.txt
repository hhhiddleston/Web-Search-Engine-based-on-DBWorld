The IDSE research centre at the Faculty of Computer Science of the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano has an open research position in the area of processing time series data, to be filled at the level of PhD or Postdoc.

More specifically, the research concentrates on:
- analyzing/mining time series data for monitoring and predicitive analytics;
- providing support for time series in database systems.

The research is in close collaboration with Oracle Research and a local company, with the possibility of an internship at Oracle Research.

We are seeking highly motivated candidates with a good background in databases and a deep interest to work on algorithmic aspects of processing and analyzing time series data.  In addition, good language skills in English are expected.

The position shall be filled asap, with a duration of up to 4 years for a PhD and 2 years for a PostDoc.

If you are interested or need more information, please contact Johann Gamper at gamper@inf.unibz.it.
