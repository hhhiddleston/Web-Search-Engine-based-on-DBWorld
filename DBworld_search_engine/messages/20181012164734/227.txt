New book from ACM Books and Morgan & Claypool:

    Declarative Logic Programming: Theory, Systems, and Applications
    Michael Kifer and Yanhong Annie Liu (eds.)

Table of contents and individual chapters can be found at
https://dl.acm.org/citation.cfm?id=3191317

This is a collection of state-of-the-art surveys on a number of key topics
in Logic Programming written by top experts in the field.
