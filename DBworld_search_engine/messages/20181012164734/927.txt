The School of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is one of the largest and most prestigious computing schools in Australia and highly ranked internationally. It offers undergraduate programs in Software Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science and Bioinformatics, as well as a number of combined degrees with other disciplines. It attracts excellent students who have an outstanding record in international competitions.
