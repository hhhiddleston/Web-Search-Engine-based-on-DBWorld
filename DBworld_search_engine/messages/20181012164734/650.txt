---------------------------------------------------------------------

                            Call for Papers 


               6th International Conference on Mining Intelligence
              and Knowledge Exploration (MIKE 2018)         
 

             December 20 - 22, 2018  -  Cluj-Napoca, Romania

                      http://www.mike.org.in/2018/ 

---------------------------------------------------------------------


The MIKE 2018 Program Committee invites submissions of original theoretical research, applied research and deployed application papers on all aspects of data mining, learning theory and knowledge discovery. Technical tracks include, but are not limited to: 
- evolutionary computation;
- knowledge exploration in Internet of Things;
- artificial intelligence;
- machine learning;
- data mining and information retrieval;
- image analysis, pattern recognition and computer vision;
- speech/signal processing;
- text mining and natural language processing;
- intelligent intelligent security systems;
- intelligent systems. 

Accepted papers will be published by Springer in LCNS/LNAI series.
Authors of accepted papers are expected to present their work at the conference.

SUBMISSION AND FORMATTING
We will adopt a double-blind review process, where the names of authors and their affiliations are unknown to reviewers until the end of the review process and are not mentioned in the paper. 

Submitted papers must contain 6 pages (short papers) and 10 pages (long papers), including text, figures and references. We require that submissions conform to the Springer LCNS format (www.springer.com/lcns).
