About the journal
https://cybersecurity.springeropen.com/about
==================================================

The new Cybersecurity journal is aimed to systematically cover all essential aspects of cybersecurity, with a focus on reporting on cyberspace security issues, the latest research results, and real-world deployment of security technologies.

This journal is the first open access journal on cybersecurity. The article processing charge (APC) will be fully covered by the Institute of Information Engineering (IIE), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). The journal is served by a dedicated international editorial board to give thorough swift editorial response (https://cybersecurity.springeropen.com/about/editorial-board).

For more details, please visit: https://cybersecurity.springeropen.com/about
