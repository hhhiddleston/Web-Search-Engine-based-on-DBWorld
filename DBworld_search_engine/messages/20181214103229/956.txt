The high-performance and data-intensive computing (HPDIC) lab at University of Nevada-Reno (UNR) has one PhD position available starting as early as January 2019. The PhD student will receive full tuition waiver plus stipend of USD $2,000/month, as a research and/or teaching assistant. More information about the lab can be found here: http://cse.unr.edu/hpdic

To apply, email the following documents to dzhao@unr.edu: 
- Resume
- Statement of Purpose, max. two pages
- Transcripts
- GRE score
- TOEFL score (for non-English speakers)
- One representative publication, if applicable

Due to the large number of requests, only applicants passing the initial screening will be contacted.
