The academic search engine and digital library CiteSeerX and related projects 
has an immediate opening for a postdoc to work on the problems of scholarly 
big data information extraction, entity recognition, indexing, and other 
related areas of research. A PhD in computer science or related fields is 
required. The position is at the College of Information Sciences and 
Technology at the Pennsylvania State University, University Park, PA. 
Familiarity with machine learning, information retrieval and extraction, 
search engines, computer systems, and linux/lucene a must. This is 
collaborative effort with Professor Jian Wu (previous posdoc) at Old 
Dominion University and Professor Cornelia Caragea at the University of 
Illinois at Chicago. 

Interested parties should contact Professor Lee Giles at giles@ist.psu.edu.
