CALL for Participation 
================================================================================
6th Workshop on Data Mining in Biomedical Informatics and Healthcare (DMBIH) '18 
Co-located with IEEE ICDM 2018
November 17th, 2018
Convention Center at Resort World Sentosa at Sentosa Island Singapore
http://facweb.cs.depaul.edu/research/vc/ICDM18/index.html
================================================================================

You are invited to attend the sixth IEEE Workshop on Data Mining in Biomedical 
Informatics and Healthcare (DMBIH) organized in conjunction with the IEEE 
International Conference on Data Mining (ICDM), Singapore, on November 17th, 
2018.  The DMBIH workshop will provide a forum for data miners, informaticists 
and clinical researchers to share latest investigations on data mining 
techniques applied to biomedical and healthcare data.

Highlights of the workshop are:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Keynote Presentation: "When AI meets healthcare practices" by Dr. Mengling 
Feng (Saw Swee Hock School of Public Health and Institute of Data Science, NUS)
- 7 peer-reviewed paper presentations 
- Invitation to submit papers to a SPECIAL ISSUE in the IEEE/ACM Transactions on 
Computational Biology and Bioinformatics (TCBB) journal

The full program is available below. For more information on the workshop, visit 
http://facweb.cs.depaul.edu/research/vc/ICDM18/schedule.pdf
