We are happy to announce the launch of the Springer journal on Quantum Machine Intelligence, the first scientific journal specifically addressing the research at the edge between quantum computing and artificial intelligence.
Expected submissions are original articles on cutting-edge experimental and theoretical research in all areas of quantum artificial intelligence, such as quantum machine learning, quantum data science and quantum computational intelligence.
Please visit the journal site https://www.springer.com/engineering/computational+intelligence+and+complexity/journal/42484
for submitting your paper.
